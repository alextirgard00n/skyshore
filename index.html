Services

In the realm of audio, specialization is my guiding principle. With an unwavering dedication to my craft, I exclusively serve metal, pop, and rock bands. These genres hold a unique place in my heart, and my focus on them enables me to deliver a level of expertise and quality that resonates with the distinct characteristics of each genre.

By narrowing my scope, I ensure that every project benefits from a tailored approach, translating into exceptional sonic experiences for musicians and audiences alike.

Mixing

Mastering

I specialize in crafting final masters that seamlessly translate to any listening system or environment.

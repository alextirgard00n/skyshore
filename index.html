About Me

Hey there, I'm Alex Tirgardoon, a seasoned musician based out of Honolulu, Hawaii and aspiring music producer with a passion for all things metal. With a drumming journey spanning two decades, I've held the rhythm for numerous bands, each experience shaping my musical evolution.

As the world embraced technology, so did I. Transitioning from drumsticks to digital audio workstations, I embarked on a new passion: producing music. Over time, I've honed my skills and proudly self-produced projects for not only my bands but also for fellow musicians. My journey led to the birth of "Skyshore Recordings," a name woven from my commitment to the 2 bands of the many that have fueled my outmost passion and creativity – Skylene and Ashore.

With a deep love for metal music, I've always aimed to find the right balance between strong, intense sounds and clear, pure tones in my mixes. Creating music that deeply connects with the essence of the genre is not just a goal, but something I'm incredibly dedicated to. Beyond all the individual sounds, I want to make sure that each song, every guitar riff, every drum hit, and even the powerful vocals stay true to their core while also sounding polished and well-crafted.
